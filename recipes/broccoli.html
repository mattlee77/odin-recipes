Broccoli

Description

Season with salt and pepper, drizzle with olive oil and stick it in the oven at 200C for about 11-12 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the broccoli into florets, making sure they are even in size.
  2. Wash and rinse, pat dry with a paper towel.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and olive oil and mix.
  4. Place on a tray and cook in an oven preheated to 200C for 11-12 minutes.
