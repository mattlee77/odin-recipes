Chicken

Description

Season to your liking and drizzle olive oil over chicken. Stick it in the oven at 210C for 30-40 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 75C.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Old Bay Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season the chicken thigh with salt, pepper and Old Bay.
  2. Put the chicken in a tray and drizzle olive oil over it on both sides.
  3. Place the tray in an oven preheated to 210C.
  4. Cook for 30-40 minutes or until internal temperature is 75C.
