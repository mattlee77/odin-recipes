Steak

Description

Steak is fairly easy to cook. Season generously and place on a searing hot pan with your preferred oil. Time and flip to the other side depending on your preferred doneness. Rest for 5 minutes and it's ready to eat.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a pan over high heat and add oil.
  3. Place the steak in the pan and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. If desired, add butter to the pan for extra flavor and baste near the end.
  5. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
